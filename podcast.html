Episode Summaries

Mar 1, 2026
Episode 8: Calm Under Pressure: Life as a Nurse Anesthetist

In this episode of Behind The Mask, CRNA Alana shares her journey from the ICU to the operating room, highlighting the critical role of managing patient safety, pain, and airway in high-stakes care. The conversation explores the precision, pressure, and mindset required to succeed in nurse anesthesia and thrive in one of healthcare’s most demanding roles.

Feb 9, 2026
Episode 7: Leading the Floor: Inside DIalysis Nursing with Charge Nurse Christina

In this episode of Behind the Mask, Charge Nurse Christina shares insight into dialysis nursing, including her daily responsibilities, leadership role, and how she supports both patients and her team. This episode also highlights her dedication as a new mom, offering an honest and personal look at balancing nursing and motherhood while inviting listeners to share feedback on the podcast.

Feb 1, 2026
Episode 6: Quiet Battles (Behind the Mask x Mind Matters)

High performance doesn’t always equal high well-being—many athletes struggle silently due to pressure, perfectionism, stigma, and fear of vulnerability. This episode highlights why speaking up is a strength, reminding athletes, coaches, and supporters that success and struggle can coexist, and mental health matters on and off the field.

Jan 26, 2026
Episode 5: Becoming an Orthopaedic Surgeon: Inside Residency with Dr. Yesha Parekh

In this episode of Behind the Mask, Dr. Yesha Parekh shares her journey through medical school and orthopaedic surgery residency, offering insight into the daily life, challenges, and rewards of physician training. The conversation highlights perseverance and provides an honest look at what it truly takes to pursue one of medicine’s most demanding specialties.

Dec 30, 2025
Episode 4: Inside Clinical Trials -- Patient Care & Research with Manda, BSN

In this episode, Manda, a clinical research nurse at the Rutgers Cancer Institute of New Jersey, shares her journey into oncology research and how clinical trials, patient care, and data intersect in her role. This conversation highlights a lesser-known but vital path in healthcare, offering valuable insight for students interested in nursing, research, and alternative medical careers.

Dec 6, 2025
Episode 3: Inside Medical Oncology & Cancer Care Leadership with Dr. Ghalib

Join us as Dr. Ghalib of the Rutgers Cancer Institute opens up about the human side of oncology administration. He discusses the challenges and rewards of supporting patients through one of the most difficult moments in their lives, his educational path to his position, and talks about how strong leadership drives better results from employees and for both patients and providers.

Nov 28, 2025
Episode 2: The Heart of Pain Management and Palliative Care with Dr. Habib

Dr. Habib, a palliative care and pain management physician at Rutgers Cancer Institute, joins us to break down what palliative care really is and how he got into one of the most meaningful branches of medicine. We talk about patient stories, pros and cons of the job, and the human side of healthcare that often goes unseen.
