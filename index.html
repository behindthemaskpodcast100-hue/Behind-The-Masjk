Our Initiative

What is Behind The Mask?

Behind The Mask is an initiative focused on early clinical exposure and patient safety education for teenagers — opening doors to the world of healthcare before most students even know those doors exist.

BTM is for students, athletes, aspiring healthcare professionals, and anyone looking for a clear, guided pathway into medicine.
